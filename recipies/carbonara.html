Spaghetti Carbonara

A rich, classic dish great to serve to company! The italian dish is quick, easy and will definately leave you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Olive oil
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • White wine
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti about 12 minutes in boiling water.
  2. Toss cooked spaghetti in olive oil.
  3. Cook bacon in skillet about 10 minutes. Leave some bacon fat in the pan for the next step.
  4. Add olive oil to bacon fat in skillet.
  5. Add onion and garlic to olive oil and bacon fat skillet.
  6. Add wine to skillet and cook for an addtional minute.
  7. Add cooked bacon and spaghetti back to skillet.
  8. Add beaten eggs to mixture, tossing with tongs until completely incorporated.
  9. Serve warm topping with parsley and parmesan.
